SEARCHED FOR: "qualia"

This is a project about the data trails we leave behind and the emotions and memories that are triggered when presented with this past data history.

All contributions are anonymous and any help is so appreciated! Please share as much or as little as you wish. Contributions will live (anonymously) on an archival page I will create for my final thesis project. The responses will be catagorized by "feeling" and "emotion."

How to download your data:

  1. Visit takeout.google.com

  2. Be sure you are logged into your personal google account, not a work or university account (any account you use most).

  3. Deselect all, scroll to "My Activity" and select it.

  4. Click "Multiple Formats" on the left.

  5. Click "HTML" and switch to "JSON."

  6. Click Next Step.

  7. Send download link via email, export once, create export.

  8. Wait for the link to come into your Google inbox.

  9. Once you recieve the .zip file, open and navigate to the "search" folder.

  10. Upload "MyActivity.json" file below.

    VIEW YOUR SEARCHES

  11. Take a minute to read through your data. Once you find a search that trigges a memory or emotion, copy and paste it into the "searched for" box. Tag it with a one word emotion from the list or create your own. Elaborate as much as you'd like to on the emotion or memory that was triggered.

  12. Once your data is entered, hit submit. Thank you for all of your help <3
